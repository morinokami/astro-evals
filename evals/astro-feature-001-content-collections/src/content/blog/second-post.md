# Understanding Content Collections

Content Collections provide a type-safe way to manage your content in Astro. They support Markdown, MDX, JSON, and YAML files with schema validation.

## Key Features

- Type-safe frontmatter with Zod validation
- Automatic TypeScript types for your content
- Built-in query APIs with getCollection and getEntry
